A. COORDINATE GEOMETRY
Q1.
What are the coordinates of the origin?
Answer: (0, 0)
Q2.
Point A(4, 0) lies on which axis?
Answer: X-axis
Q3.
Point B(0, –3) lies on which axis?
Answer: Y-axis
Q4.
Find the distance between points (2, 3) and (5, 7).
Answer: √[(5 − 2)² + (7 − 3)²] = √(9 + 16) = 5
Q5.
Find the midpoint of the line joining (2, 4) and (6, 8).
Answer: ((2 + 6)/2, (4 + 8)/2) = (4, 6)

Q6.

Answer: y = 3x + 2 → Slope = 3
Q9.

Answer: Slope = (8 − 4)/(2 − 0) = 2 → y = 2x + 4

C. PROBABILITY (BASIC)
Q11.
A coin is tossed once. What is the probability of getting heads?
Answer: 1/2
Q12.
A die is rolled. Find the probability of getting an even number.
Answer: Even = 2, 4, 6 → 3/6 = 1/2
Q13.
A bag contains 3 red and 2 blue balls. Find the probability of drawing a red ball.
Answer: 3/5
Q14.
A number is chosen from 1 to 10. Find the probability it is greater than 5.
Answer: Numbers: 6, 7, 8, 9, 10 → 5/10 = 1/2
Q15.
What is the probability of getting a vowel when a letter is chosen from the word "MATHS"?
Answer: 1 vowel (A) out of 5 letters → 1/5

Q18.

Q20.

E. QUADRATIC INTRODUCTION
Q21.
Write the general form of a quadratic equation.
Answer: ax² + bx + c = 0
Q22.
Find the discriminant of x² + 4x + 4 = 0.
Answer: D = b² − 4ac = 16 − 16 = 0
Q23.
Find the roots of x² − 5x + 6 = 0.
Answer: (x − 2)(x − 3) = 0 → x = 2, 3
Q24.
If one root of x² − 7x + 10 = 0 is 2, find the other root.
Answer: Product of roots = c/a = 10 → 2 × other = 10 → other = 5
Q25.
Determine the nature of roots for x² + 2x + 3 = 0.
Answer: D = 4 − 12 = −8 → Roots are imaginary
