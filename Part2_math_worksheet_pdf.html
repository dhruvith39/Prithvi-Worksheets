A. DIRECT & INVERSE PROPORTION
Q1.
If 5 pencils cost $10, how much will 8 pencils cost?
Answer: 10/5 = $2 per pencil → 8 × 2 = $16
Q2.
x and y are directly proportional. If x = 4 when y = 12, find y when x = 10.
Answer: y₁/x₁ = y₂/x₂ → 12/4 = y/10 → y = 30
Q3.
x and y are inversely proportional. If x = 3, y = 8, find y when x = 6.
Answer: x₁y₁ = x₂y₂ → 3 × 8 = 6 × y → y = 4
Q4.
A car travels 120 km in 3 hours. How long will it take to travel 200 km at the same speed?
Answer: Speed = 40 km/h → Time = 200 ÷ 40 = 5 hours
Q5.
The time to complete a job is inversely proportional to the number of workers. If 6 workers take 8 days, how many days will 12 workers take?
Answer: 6 × 8 = 12 × d → d = 4 days
B. ANGLES & LINES
Q6.
Find the complement of 65°.
Answer: 90° − 65° = 25°
Q7.
Find the supplement of 125°.
Answer: 180° − 125° = 55°
Q8.
If two angles form a linear pair and one angle is 70°, find the other.
Answer: 180° − 70° = 110°
Q9.
If two parallel lines are cut by a transversal, then alternate interior angles are _____.
Answer: Equal
Q10.
If two lines intersect, the vertically opposite angles are _____.
Answer: Equal
C. TRIANGLES
Q11.
The sum of all angles of a triangle is _____.
Answer: 180°
Q12.
In an equilateral triangle, each angle measures _____.
Answer: 60°
Q13.
The sides of a triangle are 5 cm, 12 cm, and 13 cm. Check if it is a right triangle.
Answer: 5² + 12² = 13² → 25 + 144 = 169 → Yes, right triangle
Q14.
A triangle has sides 6 cm, 8 cm, 10 cm. Find its perimeter.
Answer: 6 + 8 + 10 = 24 cm
Q15.
Two angles of a triangle are 45° and 55°. Find the third angle.
Answer: 180 − (45 + 55) = 80°
D. QUADRILATERALS
Q16.
The sum of all angles of a quadrilateral is _____.
Answer: 360°
Q17.
The opposite sides of a parallelogram are ____ and ____.
Answer: Equal and parallel
Q18.
In a rectangle, each angle is _____.
Answer: 90°
Q19.
In a rhombus, all sides are _____.
Answer: Equal
Q20.
In a square, all sides are equal and all angles are _____.
Answer: 90°
E. POLYGONS
Q21.
The sum of interior angles of a polygon with n sides = ?
Answer: (n − 2) × 180°
Q22.
Find the sum of interior angles of a hexagon.
Answer: (6 − 2) × 180 = 720°
Q23.
Find each interior angle of a regular pentagon.
Answer: (5 − 2) × 180 / 5 = 108°
Q24.
How many sides does a polygon have if the sum of interior angles is 900°?
Answer: (n − 2) × 180 = 900 → n − 2 = 5 → n = 7
Q25.
Name a polygon with all sides equal and all angles equal.
Answer: Regular Polygon
