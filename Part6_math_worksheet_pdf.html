TOPIC 2: COMPLETING THE SQUARE & TURNING POINT
Q11.
Write in completed square form: x² + 6x + 5
Answer: (x + 3)² − 4
Q12.
Find the turning point of y = x² + 6x + 5
Answer: Vertex = (−3, −4)
Q13.
Complete the square: x² − 8x + 3
Answer: (x − 4)² − 13
Q14.
Find minimum value of y = x² − 6x + 11
Answer: y = (x − 3)² + 2 → Minimum = 2 at x = 3
Q15.
Find the turning point of y = 2x² − 4x + 1
Answer: x = −b/2a = 4/4 = 1, y = 2(1)² − 4(1) + 1 = −1, Turning point = (1, −1)
