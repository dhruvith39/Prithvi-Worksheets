A. CIRCLES
Q1.
The radius of a circle is 7 cm. Find its circumference.
Answer: 2πr = 2 × 3.14 × 7 = 43.96 cm
Q2.
Find the area of a circle of radius 10 cm.
Answer: πr² = 3.14 × 10² = 314 cm²
Q3.
The diameter of a circle is 14 cm. Find its radius.
Answer: r = 14/2 = 7 cm
Q4.
Find the length of the arc that subtends an angle of 90° at the center of a circle of radius 8 cm.
Answer: 90/360 × 2πr = 1/4 × 2 × 3.14 × 8 = 12.56 cm
Q5.
The area of a circle is 154 cm². Find its radius (take π = 3.14).
Answer: r = √(154/3.14) ≈ 7 cm
B. AREA & PERIMETER (2D SHAPES)
Q6.
Find the area of a rectangle of length 12 cm and breadth 8 cm.
Answer: 12 × 8 = 96 cm²
Q7.
Find the perimeter of a square whose side is 9 cm.
Answer: 4 × 9 = 36 cm
Q8.
Find the area of a triangle with base 10 cm and height 6 cm.
Answer: 1/2 × 10 × 6 = 30 cm²
Q9.
Find the area of a parallelogram with base 15 cm and height 9 cm.
Answer: 15 × 9 = 135 cm²
Q10.
Find the perimeter of a regular hexagon whose side is 7 cm.
Answer: 6 × 7 = 42 cm
C. SURFACE AREA & VOLUME (3D SHAPES)
Q11.
Find the volume of a cube of side 5 cm.
Answer: 5³ = 125 cm³
Q12.
Find the total surface area of a cube of side 10 cm.
Answer: 6a² = 6 × 10² = 600 cm²
Q13.
Find the volume of a cuboid of length 8 cm, breadth 5 cm, and height 4 cm.
Answer: 8 × 5 × 4 = 160 cm³
Q14.
Find the volume of a cylinder of radius 7 cm and height 10 cm.
Answer: πr²h = 3.14 × 7² × 10 = 1538.6 cm³
Q15.
Find the curved surface area of a cylinder of radius 7 cm and height 10 cm.
Answer: 2πrh = 2 × 3.14 × 7 × 10 = 439.6 cm²
D. LINEAR INEQUALITIES
Q16.
Solve and represent on number line: x + 3 < 7
Answer: x < 4
Q17.
Solve: 2x − 5 ≥ 9
Answer: 2x ≥ 14 → x ≥ 7
Q18.
Solve: −3x ≤ 9
Answer: x ≥ −3 (sign changes when dividing by a negative)
Q19.
Solve: 5x + 2 > 12
Answer: 5x > 10 → x > 2
Q20.
If x is an integer satisfying 2 < x ≤ 6, list possible values.
Answer: 3, 4, 5, 6
E. DATA HANDLING & STATISTICS
Q21.
The marks of 5 students are: 10, 20, 30, 40, 50. Find the mean.
Answer: (10 + 20 + 30 + 40 + 50)/5 = 30
Q22.
Find the median of the data: 2, 4, 6, 8, 10.
Answer: 6
Q23.
Find the mode of the data: 3, 4, 4, 5, 6, 6, 6.
Answer: 6
Q24.
The average age of 5 students is 15 years. Find their total age.
Answer: 15 × 5 = 75 years
Q25.
A pie chart shows that 25% of a class chose Science, 35% chose Commerce, and the rest chose Arts. What percentage chose Arts?
Answer: 100 − (25 + 35) = 40%
