A. MENSURATION – ADVANCED 3D SHAPES
Q1.
Find the volume of a sphere of radius 7 cm.
Answer: 4/3 πr³ = 4/3 × 3.14 × 7³ = 1436.76 cm³
Q2.
Find the surface area of a sphere of radius 7 cm.
Answer: 4πr² = 4 × 3.14 × 49 = 615.44 cm²
Q3.
Find the volume of a cone of radius 3 cm and height 9 cm.
Answer: 1/3 πr²h = 1/3 × 3.14 × 9 × 9 = 84.78 cm³
Q4.
Find the curved surface area of a cone with radius 7 cm and slant height 10 cm.
Answer: πrl = 3.14 × 7 × 10 = 219.8 cm²
Q5.
Find the total surface area of a hemisphere of radius 5 cm.
Answer: 3πr² = 3 × 3.14 × 25 = 235.5 cm²
B. STATISTICS – TABLES & DATA
Q6.
The marks of students are given below. Find the mean marks.
Marks 10 20 30 40 50
No. of Students 2 4 6 5 3
Answer: (10×2 + 20×4 + 30×6 + 40×5 + 50×3)/20 = 720/20 = 36
Q7.
Find the mode from the same data.
Answer: Highest frequency = 6 (for 30 marks) → Mode = 30
Q8.
Find the median class.
Answer: Total = 20 → Median = 10th and 11th → lies in 30 → Median = 30
Q9.
The average weight of 10 students is 45 kg. Find the total weight.
Answer: 45 × 10 = 450 kg
Q10.
The mean of 5 numbers is 18. If one number is 28, find the mean of remaining 4 numbers.
Answer: Sum = 18 × 5 = 90, Remaining = 90 − 28 = 62, Mean = 62 ÷ 4 = 15.5

Q14.

Q15.

Q21.
A cylindrical water tank has radius 3 m and height 5 m. Find its capacity in liters.
Answer: Volume = πr²h = 3.14 × 9 × 5 = 141.3 m³ → 141.3 × 1000 = 141,300 liters
Q22.

Answer: √(10² − 8²) = √36 = 6 m
Q23.

Answer: 2πr = 2 × 3.14 × 7 = 43.96 m
